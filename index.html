This frog is a long way from home. Can you help it make its way back? Using only your arrow keys, you must guide the frog across the busy roads, taking care not to be run over by the cars zipping past. Then, you must help it cross the treacherous river by hopping on to the backs of the logs.

The lilypads floating in the far pond are the frog's home. To win the game you must successfully navigate the frog on to one of these lilypads. You only have 30 seconds in which to do this. When the time runs out, the game is over.

Collect these sunflowers as you travel to gain points - each sunflower is worth 5 points. But be careful! If you are hit by one of the cars you will lose 5 points and if you fall in to any water you will lose 2 points.
